Card Title

Instructions:

Download this repository to your computer.

Keep track of (roughly) how long this takes you to complete from start to finish.

Use Flexbox only. (No Floats or Grid.)

Modify only styles.css and make the following changes:

  1. For mobile views (device widths of 768px and above), display two <section class="card">s per row.
  2. For desktop views (device widths of 1024px and above), display three <section class="card">s per row.
  3. Make sure that there is adequate horizontal spacing between the cards when they are placed side by side.
  4. Set a max-width of 1280px on the body to prevent everything from looking too stretched out on extra large screen sizes.

When you're finished, upload your project to GitHub and reply to the original assessment email with a link to your repository and the time it took you to complete.

Notes:

  • All the media queries that you will need are already in styles.css. You do not need to add any other media queries.
  • Don't overthink this: It can be done in fewer than 10 lines of code.
  • Need a Flexbox reminder? A Complete Guide to Flexbox
  • Don't ask for help on this one. We want to see if you can do this on your own.
  • If it's taking you beyond 1 hour to complete, stop there, reply to the email, and let me know.
